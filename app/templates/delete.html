Data Deletion Instructions (Nxt Call)

Last Updated: January 14, 2026

This page explains how you can request deletion of your data from Nxt Call (“App”, “we”, “our”, “us”). These instructions are provided to support Facebook/Meta App Verification.

App Name: Nxt Call
Business URL: https://crm-2gkk.onrender.com/
Contact Email: support@nxtcall.app

1) How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your Nxt Call account data at any time by email.

Step-by-step:

  1. Send an email to support@nxtcall.app
  2. Use the subject line: “Data Deletion Request – Nxt Call”
  3. In the email body, include:
    • Your registered email address or mobile number used in Nxt Call
    • Your full name (optional but helpful for verification)
    • If applicable, mention that the request relates to Facebook/Meta data

We may ask for additional information only to verify that the request is genuinely from the account owner.

2) Facebook/Meta Data Deletion Request (If You Connected Facebook)

If you logged in with Facebook or granted Meta permissions, you can request deletion of Facebook-related data as well.

Step-by-step:

  1. Email support@nxtcall.app
  2. Use the subject line: “Facebook Data Deletion Request – Nxt Call”
  3. Include:
    • Your Nxt Call account email/phone
    • Any relevant Facebook identifiers you can provide (optional), such as:
      • Facebook profile link, or
      • Facebook App-scoped User ID (if available)

3) What We Delete

When your deletion request is approved, we will delete or permanently anonymize (where deletion is not technically feasible) the following, as applicable:

  • Your Nxt Call account profile information
  • Data you created or stored in the app (e.g., leads/contacts, notes, logs)
  • Facebook/Meta data received through integrations (if applicable)
  • Associated identifiers linked to your account

Exceptions: We may retain limited information if required by law, security needs, fraud prevention, or to comply with platform and legal obligations.

4) Processing Time

We aim to process valid deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe, typically 7–30 days, depending on request complexity and legal requirements.

5) Confirmation

Once the deletion request is completed, we will send a confirmation email to the address used to submit the request.
